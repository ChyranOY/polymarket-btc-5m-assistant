    [ NO TRADES ]

    One entry path. The bot only enters when the market thinks the answer is mostly known AND BTC spot agrees.

    FAVORITE

    Favorite + spot confirmation

    Buys whichever side of the market is the favorite (highest priced) once its ask is in the 0.75 – 0.97 band, and BTC spot has moved in that direction over the last 30 seconds. Two independent signals must agree before a trade fires.

    • Pick favorite: side with the higher ask (closer to $1).
    • Price gate: 0.75 ≤ ask ≤ 0.97. Below 0.75 is too uncertain; above 0.97 leaves no profit room for the SL risk.
    • Spot gate: 30-second BTC change must agree with the side. UP needs delta > 0; DOWN needs delta < 0. Flat or missing data = skip.
    • Standard guards still apply: spread ≤ MAX_ENTRY_SPREAD, ≥ TIME_LEFT_MIN_MINUTES left, no open position, cooldown clear, in trading hours.
    SHARED

    Exit rules

    • Stop-loss: exits at −STOP_LOSS_PCT × contract_size (currently −30%).
    • No take-profit — winners ride to settlement. Removed 2026-04-27 because the trailer was cutting wins to half the size of an average loss.
    • Market rollover: position closes at settlement when the 5-minute market ends.
    • Kill switch: manual exit via the control endpoint.
    RETIRED

    Cheap side & momentum (2026-04-28)

    The cheap-side value bet (0.15–0.45 range) and the momentum-chase ($50 in 2 min) were both removed when we switched to the favorite-with-confirmation model. Historical trades still carry their original strategy tag.
