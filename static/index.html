    [ NO TRADES ]

    The bot can enter a trade through one of two paths. Each trade in the Recent trades list is tagged with which one fired.

    CHEAP

    Cheap side

    Buys whichever side of the market is priced between CHEAP_SIDE_MIN and CHEAP_SIDE_MAX. A value bet — if the "cheap" side is mispriced, winning pays more than losing costs.

    • Default range: 0.15 – 0.45
    • Needs at least TIME_LEFT_MIN_MINUTES (1.5 min) left in the market
    • Spread must be under MAX_ENTRY_SPREAD (0.04)
    • Uses Kelly sizing when KELLY_ENABLED=true, otherwise flat STAKE_PCT.
    MOMENTUM

    Momentum

    Bets the direction BTC just moved. When Coinbase spot shifts hard in two minutes (±$100), the 5-min Polymarket contract rarely reverses in time.

    • Threshold: absolute BTC change ≥ $50 over the trailing 120 seconds
    • Ignores cheap_side bounds — momentum trades can enter at any price the spread gate allows
    • Still respects the spread gate and all base skip reasons (cooldown, position-open, etc.)
    • Direction: UP if spot went up, DOWN if it went down
    EXIT RULE

    Ride ITM to settlement

    Independent of strategy — applies to every open position. When a winning trade is almost certain to settle at $1.00, the trailing take-profit is suspended so the position rides all the way home instead of bailing at 70-90% of peak.

    • Triggers when mark ≥ 0.90 AND less than 60 seconds remain
    • Stop-loss still fires on catastrophic reversals
    SHARED

    Exit rules (all trades)

    • Stop-loss: exits at −STOP_LOSS_PCT × contract_size (currently −30%).
    • Trailing take-profit: arms once peak PnL reaches TAKE_PROFIT_ACTIVATION_PCT × contract_size (10%), then exits if PnL retraces below TAKE_PROFIT_GIVEBACK_PCT × peak (currently 90%).
    • Market rollover: if the current 5-minute market ends, the position closes at settlement.
    • Kill switch: manual exit via the control endpoint.
